## The Core Claim

Reality is a **3D boundary-less holographic network-field** with two inseparable aspects:

1. **Network-Field Geometry** (static aspect): The structure of all possible correlations—pure potential
2. **Thermodynamic Processing** (dynamic aspect): The actualization of those correlations—pure becoming

These aren't two different systems. They're the same reality viewed from different perspectives—like how a river is both "the shape of the riverbed" (geometry) and "the flowing water" (process). Neither exists without the other.

The system drives itself through continuous feedback:

**Geometry sets possible paths** → **Processing actualizes along those paths** → **Actualization reshapes geometry** → **New paths become possible** → *repeat forever*

No external driver needed. No outside. No beginning or end to this loop. Reality is this loop.

## What "Holographic" Means Here

Every region of space contains an encoded description of the correlation patterns with all other regions. This isn't projection from an external boundary (like AdS/CFT). The encoding IS the geometric structure itself.

**Why this matters**: When something happens anywhere, it's immediately reflected everywhere—not because information travels, but because every region's description already referenced every other region. Change the correlation locally, and the globally-encoded pattern updates everywhere instantly.

Think of it like: In a hologram, every piece contains information about the whole image. In HNet, every region contains correlation information about the whole universe. But there's no external holographic plate—the encoding is intrinsic to the network-field geometry.

## The Two Processing Modes

Here's where the Higgs field enters:

### Massive Particles (Higgs-Coupled)
- Have mass = continuously interact with the omnipresent Higgs field
- Continuous interaction = **continuous processing**
- Continuous processing = **continuous time flow**
- Experience time dilation in gravity fields

### Massless Particles (Non-Higgs-Coupled)
- No mass = no interaction with Higgs field
- Only interact during discrete events (emission/absorption)
- Discrete events = **event-time** (not continuous flow)
- Don't experience time dilation (no continuous time to dilate)

**The mechanism is simple**: The Higgs field is everywhere. Having mass means constantly interacting with it. Constant interaction = continuous processing. No mass = no constant interaction = only discrete event processing.

## How Quantum Mechanics Emerges

### Superposition
The network-field geometry can hold multiple compatible configurations simultaneously when it's **under-constrained**—when the potential structure hasn't been fully actualized yet.

These aren't probabilities or hidden variables. They're real, co-existing correlation patterns in the geometric potential.

### Measurement/Collapse
Thermodynamic processing actualizes one specific configuration from the under-constrained possibilities.

**Why massive objects cause collapse**: Massive particles do continuous processing (constant Higgs interaction). When a superposed system interacts with massive matter, it gets pulled into continuous processing and must resolve to a single actualized state.

Isolated massless systems can maintain superposition indefinitely because they only process during discrete events.

### Entanglement
Two particles become entangled when a single thermodynamic event actualizes their geometry together, creating a shared correlation pattern.

This correlation isn't stored "between" them. It's encoded in every region's description of both particles (holographic encoding).

When you measure particle A:
- You force a local geometric actualization
- Because the correlation is globally encoded everywhere
- Particle B's geometry is instantly constrained to match
- No information travels; the correlation was already written into the fabric everywhere

**No spooky action**: Nothing propagates. The correlation structure was already redundantly encoded throughout space. Measurement just selects which globally consistent configuration gets actualized.

## How Particles Emerge

Particles are **stable topological configurations** (knots/twists) in the network-field geometry.

**Why they persist**: Topological stability. A knot can't untie without breaking the rope. Similarly, these geometric configurations can't dissolve without violating the network-field's structural constraints.

**Why specific particles exist**: The network-field substrate only allows certain stable topological configurations. The Standard Model particle zoo represents the complete set of configurations that can remain stable under thermodynamic processing.

Different knot types = different particles (electrons, quarks, photons, etc.)

**Classical spacetime** = heavily decohered network-field geometry

## Why Time, Entropy, and Causality Work

### Time Flow
Thermodynamic processing IS time. For massive particles, continuous Higgs interaction = continuous actualization = experienced as continuous time flow. For massless particles, discrete events = discrete time.

The "arrow of time" is the direction of actualization.

### Entropy Increase
Actualization moves from under-constrained (many possible configurations) to constrained (one actualized configuration). This IS entropy increase: geometric freedom decreases, definiteness increases.

Thermodynamic processing always actualizes (never "un-actualizes"), so entropy always increases.

### Past vs. Future
- **Past**: Already-actualized configurations (fixed, definite)
- **Future**: Still-potential configurations (open, under-constrained)
- **Present**: The ongoing actualization event

## What About Gravitational Redshift?

Photons show gravitational redshift, but this doesn't contradict the event-time concept.

**Massive observers** at different gravitational potentials experience different continuous time flow rates (time dilation).

**Photons** traveling between those observers undergo frequency shifts because they're moving through regions of different gravitational potential (geometric energy changes).

But the photon itself doesn't "experience" this as time dilation—it operates in event-time (emission event → absorption event). The frequency shift is purely geometric from the photon's perspective.

All particles warp space (create gravity). Only Higgs-coupled particles experience time flow dilation.
